Who is Isidoro?

Isidoro Sofia, known online as @isidaurum, is a student originally from Palermo and currently based in Turin, where he studies Biology (as a doctor in Faunistic Science).

With a strong passion for photography and urban videography, Isidoro shares personal and intimate stories of Turin through his active presence on Instagram, TikTok, Threads and Facebook.

Self-described as a "Fuorisede in Turin" (a student living away from home), he offers a fresh and authentic perspective on city life. His engaging visual storytelling captures the everyday moments and unique atmosphere of Turin, attracting a steadily growing audience of young creatives, students, and urban explorers eager to discover the city through his eyes.
